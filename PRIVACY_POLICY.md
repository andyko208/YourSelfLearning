# Privacy Policy for YourSelfLearning Browser Extension

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 15, 2025

## Overview

YourSelfLearning is a browser extension designed to transform social media scrolling into learning opportunities by presenting educational quiz lessons at configurable intervals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our extension.

## Information We Collect

### Data Collected and Stored Locally

YourSelfLearning stores the following data **locally on your device only** using your browser's local storage:

**Usage Metrics:**
- Scroll count on tracked social media platforms
- Time spent on tracked platforms (organized by 8-hour periods: morning, afternoon, evening)
- Number of educational lessons completed
- Daily comparison data (today vs. yesterday)

**Settings and Preferences:**
- Enabled social media platforms for tracking
- Lesson frequency preferences (often/sometimes/barely)
- Selected educational themes and topics
- Brain battery level (gamification metric)

**Educational Content Tracking:**
- Lesson completion status and timing
- Selected lesson themes and topics
- Random interval tracking for bonus notifications

### Data We Do NOT Collect

YourSelfLearning does **NOT** collect, transmit, or store:
- Personal identifying information
- Browsing history outside of scroll tracking on specified platforms
- Content of websites you visit
- Login credentials or passwords
- Email addresses or contact information
- Financial information
- Location data
- Search queries or typed content

## How We Use Your Data

All data collected by YourSelfLearning is used exclusively for:

1. **Functionality:** Tracking your scrolling behavior and time spent on enabled platforms
2. **Educational Intervention:** Triggering lessons at appropriate intervals based on your activity
3. **Progress Tracking:** Displaying your daily metrics and comparing with previous days
4. **Personalization:** Customizing lesson content based on your theme and topic preferences
5. **Gamification:** Managing the "brain battery" system to encourage learning

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage Only:** All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's local storage API
- **No Remote Transmission:** Your data never leaves your device or gets transmitted to external servers
- **No Third-Party Access:** No third parties have access to your usage data
- **User Control:** You can clear all extension data by removing the extension or clearing browser data

## Third-Party Services

YourSelfLearning may access external educational resources through "Learn More" links that direct you to reputable educational websites. When you click these links:
- You will be redirected to external websites not controlled by YourSelfLearning
- Those websites have their own privacy policies
- We do not track or monitor your activity on external websites
- No data is shared with these external websites

## Permissions and Their Purpose

YourSelfLearning requests the following browser permissions:

- **Storage:** To save your settings, metrics, and preferences locally
- **Active Tab:** To detect when you're on tracked social media platforms
- **Host Permissions (Social Media Sites):** To monitor scrolling activity and inject educational content on:
  - Instagram (*.instagram.com/*)
  - TikTok (*.tiktok.com/*)
  - YouTube (*.youtube.com/*)
  - X/Twitter (*.x.com/*)
  - Reddit (*.reddit.com/*)
  - Facebook (*.facebook.com/*)
  - Amazon (*.amazon.com/*)

These permissions are used solely for the core functionality described in this policy.

## Children's Privacy

YourSelfLearning is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The educational content provided is designed for general audiences.

## Data Retention

- **Local Data:** Stored indefinitely until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data
- **Daily Rollover:** Metrics reset daily at local midnight with yesterday's data preserved for comparison
- **User Control:** You can reset all data through the extension's settings or by uninstalling

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access:** View all your data through the extension's interface
- **Modification:** Change settings and preferences at any time
- **Deletion:** Clear all data by uninstalling the extension
- **Platform Selection:** Choose which social media platforms to track
- **Content Selection:** Customize educational themes and topics
